About the Event

The **Newly Mini Hackathon** was a high-energy, single-day sprint held at ESU Jaffna, bringing together over 100 students to explore the intersection of AI and No-Code development. The event was designed to demystify complex technologies and empower the next generation of builders to create functional applications in record time.

Participants were challenged to identify real-world problems and prototype solutions using AI agents and modern No-Code platforms. The vibe was electric, with mentors providing hands-on guidance and students pushing the boundaries of their creativity.
