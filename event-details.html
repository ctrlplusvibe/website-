About the Event

The Newly Mini Hackathon: Build Your First Mobile App was a focused physical technology event hosted with Newly and ESU Jaffna in Jaffna, Sri Lanka. Across a fast-paced four-hour format, 109 students learned how AI-powered development and No-Code tools can turn a product idea into a working mobile app prototype without requiring a traditional coding background.

The event connected local student talent with global builder skills: rapid prototyping, AI-assisted product thinking, mobile app design, live demos, hands-on building, and pitch-style showcases. By making modern app creation more accessible, the Newly Mini Hackathon supported a practical ESG social-impact goal: improving AI literacy, youth employability, and inclusive digital education in the Northern Province tech ecosystem.

For international readers, this Jaffna hackathon is a clear example of how regional communities can participate in the global AI and No-Code movement. Students explored real-world problems, used Newly to accelerate mobile app creation, and gained confidence to build, test, and present solutions that can scale beyond one classroom or one country.
